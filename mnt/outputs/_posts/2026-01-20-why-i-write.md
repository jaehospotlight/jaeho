Writing is thinking made visible. When you write, you're forced to take the tangled mess of half-formed ideas in your head and give them structure. You discover what you actually believe versus what you only thought you believed.

I started writing not to build an audience, but to build clarity. Every post is a chance to stress-test an idea — to see if it holds up when you lay it out in full sentences instead of bullet points in a notes app.
