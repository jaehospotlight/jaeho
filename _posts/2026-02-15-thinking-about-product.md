The best products feel obvious in hindsight. They make you wonder why nobody built them sooner. But getting to that feeling of inevitability requires an enormous amount of deliberate work.

Great products are opinionated. They make strong choices about what to include and, more importantly, what to leave out. Every feature is a trade-off, and the best product teams understand that saying no is usually more valuable than saying yes.
